<p><p>Composite gas pressure vessels are designed to store gases under high pressure, utilizing advanced materials for enhanced safety and performance. Type III vessels are typically made with a metal liner encased in a composite material, offering a balance of weight and durability. Type IV vessels, on the other hand, feature a fully plastic liner with a composite shell, providing even greater lightweight characteristics and resistance to corrosion. Both types cater to various applications, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial gas storage.</p></p>
