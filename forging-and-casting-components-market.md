<p><p>The forging and casting components market involves the production of metal parts through two primary processes. Forging involves shaping metal using compressive forces, resulting in strong, durable components ideal for high-stress applications. Common methods include open-die, closed-die, and drop forging. In contrast, casting involves pouring molten metal into molds to create complex shapes, offering design flexibility. This method is suitable for low-volume production and intricate geometries. Both processes are critical in manufacturing industries, catering to diverse applications from automotive to aerospace.</p></p>
