<p><ul><li>Side Door DCU</li><li>Power Lift Gate DCU</li><li>Anti-trap Window DCU</li><li>Traditional Non-anti-trap Window DCU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Door Control Unit (DCU) market encompasses various types designed for specific functionalities. Side Door DCUs manage the locking and unlocking of side doors, enhancing convenience and security. Power Lift Gate DCUs facilitate automated opening and closing of rear hatches for easier access. Anti-trap Window DCUs employ sensors to prevent injuries by reversing window operation if an obstruction is detected. Traditional Non-anti-trap Window DCUs operate basic window functions without safety sensors, catering to cost-sensitive consumers.</p></p>
