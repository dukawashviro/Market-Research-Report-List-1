<p><strong>The Aircraft Cooling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cycle Air Conditioning</li><li>Vapor Cycle Air Conditioning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft cooling systems market comprises primarily two types: Air Cycle Air Conditioning and Vapor Cycle Air Conditioning. Air Cycle systems utilize ambient air to cool cabin temperatures, circulating it through heat exchangers and expanding it to remove heat. In contrast, Vapor Cycle systems use refrigerants that change states between liquid and gas, absorbing and releasing heat to provide effective cooling. Both systems are crucial for maintaining passenger comfort and operational efficiency in varying flight conditions.</p></p>
