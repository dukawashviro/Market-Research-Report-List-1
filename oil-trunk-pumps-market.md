<p><ul><li>Tube Pump</li><li>Rod Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-trunk pumps market primarily consists of tube pumps and rod pumps, both crucial for fluid transfer in various applications. Tube pumps use a flexible tube to create a peristaltic action, ideal for transporting corrosive or sensitive liquids. In contrast, rod pumps rely on a mechanical rod system to lift oil from well depths, commonly utilized in oil extraction industries. Each type serves distinct operational needs, providing efficient solutions depending on the specific requirements of the pumping process.</p></p>
