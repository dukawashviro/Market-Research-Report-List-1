<p><p>The PE/PET bi-component fiber market is segmented into staple fiber and filament types. In contrast, filament fibers are continuous strands that provide strength and a smooth texture, making them ideal for applications like sportswear and industrial textiles. Both types leverage the benefits of polyethylene and polyester, offering versatility in production and enhanced performance characteristics in various end products.</p></p>
