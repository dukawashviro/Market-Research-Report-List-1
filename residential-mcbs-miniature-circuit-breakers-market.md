<p><p>The residential MCB market is categorized into various types based on the number of poles. Single-pole MCBs control individual circuits, suitable for lighting and small appliances. Two-pole MCBs are designed for 230V circuits, typically used in residential applications like kitchen equipment. Three-pole MCBs handle three-phase systems, often found in larger homes with heavy machinery. Four-pole MCBs cater to three-phase systems with neutral, providing comprehensive protection for complex electrical setups. Each type serves distinct needs for safety and efficiency in residential settings.</p></p>
