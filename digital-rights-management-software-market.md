<p><p>The Digital Rights Management (DRM) Software market is categorized into two primary types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise DRM involves software installed directly on a company's servers, providing control over data and compliance but requiring significant maintenance and IT resources. In contrast, Cloud-Based DRM offers scalability and accessibility through the internet, allowing businesses to manage rights and protect content without heavy infrastructure. Each type serves different organizational needs, balancing control, cost, and ease of deployment.</p></p>
