<p><p>The Whole Body Contamination Monitor market features two primary types of detectors: Plastic Scintillation Detectors and Gas Flow Proportional Detectors. Plastic Scintillation Detectors utilize plastic materials that emit light when radiation interacts, allowing for effective detection of beta and gamma radiation. In contrast, Gas Flow Proportional Detectors use a gas-filled chamber to detect radiation through ionization, providing high sensitivity and energy resolution. Both types serve critical roles in monitoring radioactive contamination in medical, industrial, and research settings.</p></p>
