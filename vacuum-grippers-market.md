<p><p>The vacuum grippers market is primarily divided into two types: Compressed Air-type Vacuum Grippers and Electromechanical-driven Vacuum Grippers. Compressed Air-type Grippers utilize pneumatic pressure to create suction, making them suitable for high-speed applications and diverse materials. In contrast, Electromechanical-driven Vacuum Grippers employ electric motors to generate suction, offering greater precision and control, ideal for sensitive or complex handling tasks. Both types enhance automation in various industries by facilitating efficient material handling and boosting productivity.</p></p>
