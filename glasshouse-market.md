<p><p>Glasshouse market types include hoop houses and dome houses, both designed for controlled agricultural environments. Hoop houses feature a simple, arch-like structure covered with plastic, providing cost-effective protection for crops while extending growing seasons. In contrast, dome houses have a geodesic design that maximizes space and light, promoting efficient airflow and temperature regulation. Each type serves to enhance plant growth, protect against harsh weather, and improve yields, appealing to both commercial growers and hobby gardeners seeking innovative farming solutions.</p></p>
