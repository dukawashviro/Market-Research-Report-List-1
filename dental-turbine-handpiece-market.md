<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust Handpiece</li><li>Outside Exhaust Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental turbine handpiece market includes inside exhaust and outside exhaust types, differentiated by their air exhaust systems. Inside exhaust handpieces expel air through the handpiece's interior, leading to a quieter operation and better visibility during procedures. Conversely, outside exhaust handpieces release air externally, which can help in cooling but may generate more noise. Each type has its specific applications and preferences among dental professionals, influencing their adoption based on functionality, comfort, and procedural requirements.</p></p>
