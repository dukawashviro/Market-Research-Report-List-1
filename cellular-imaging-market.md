<p><ul><li>Microscopic Techniques</li><li>Macroscopic Techniques</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellular imaging market features two primary types: microscopic and macroscopic techniques. Microscopic techniques involve high-resolution imaging methods, such as fluorescence and electron microscopy, enabling detailed visualization of cellular structures and processes at the micro-level. Conversely, macroscopic techniques focus on imaging larger tissue sections or whole organisms, using methods like imaging mass spectrometry and optical coherence tomography. Both approaches are vital for research and diagnostics, providing insights into cellular functions, disease mechanisms, and therapeutic developments.</p></p>
