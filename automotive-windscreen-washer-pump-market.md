<p><p>The automotive windscreen washer pump market is primarily divided into two types: mono pumps and dual pumps. Mono pumps are single-unit systems that efficiently serve basic windshield cleaning needs, typically found in standard vehicles. In contrast, dual pumps have two separate units that allow for more advanced functionality, such as simultaneous cleaning of front and rear windshields or enhanced fluid distribution. The choice between them depends on vehicle type, performance requirements, and consumer preferences for cleaning efficiency and versatility.</p></p>
