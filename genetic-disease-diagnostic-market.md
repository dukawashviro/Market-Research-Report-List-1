<p><p>The genetic disease diagnostic market encompasses various types of testing to identify genetic disorders. Prenatal diagnosis involves testing fetuses for genetic anomalies before birth, providing valuable information for expecting parents. Screening for single gene genetic diseases focuses on detecting specific inherited conditions, allowing for early intervention. Preimplantation genetic testing assesses embryos during in vitro fertilization, helping select those free of genetic disorders. Additionally, the "others" category includes diverse diagnostic methods such as carrier screening and expanded carrier screening for broader genetic risk assessment.</p></p>
