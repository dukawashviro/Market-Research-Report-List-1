<p><p>Faux leather for furniture consists of various synthetic materials designed to mimic genuine leather's appearance and feel. PVC leather, made from polyvinyl chloride, is durable and water-resistant but less breathable. PU leather, crafted from polyurethane, offers a softer, more flexible alternative with better breathability and eco-friendliness. Other types may include microfiber or bonded leather, which combines genuine leather fibers with synthetic materials, providing a stylish yet affordable option for furniture upholstery in diverse settings.</p></p>
