<p><p>The primary care EHR software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EHR systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, automatic updates, and reduced IT overhead. Conversely, on-premise EHR software is installed locally on a healthcare provider's servers, providing enhanced data control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different organizational needs and preferences in managing patient information.</p></p>
