<p><strong>The Laryngeal Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hood Laryngeal Stent</li><li>Tubular Laryngeal Stent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laryngeal stents market primarily comprises two types: hood laryngeal stents and tubular laryngeal stents. Hood laryngeal stents are designed to cover and protect the laryngeal area, facilitating ventilation and maintaining airway patency while providing an anatomical shape. In contrast, tubular laryngeal stents serve as rigid conduits, ensuring unobstructed airflow through the larynx and bronchial passages. Both types play crucial roles in managing airway issues, particularly in patients with laryngeal obstruction or requiring surgical interventions.</p></p>
