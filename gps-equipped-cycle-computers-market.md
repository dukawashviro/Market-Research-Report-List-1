<p><p>The GPS-equipped cycle computers market is categorized into three main types: wired computers, wireless computers, and wireless GPS computers. Wired computers connect directly to the bicycle's frame, providing reliable data transmission but limited flexibility. Wireless computers, while easier to install and move, may face signal interference. Wireless GPS computers combine GPS functionality with wireless technology, offering advanced features like route tracking, speed monitoring, and elevation data, making them popular among cyclists seeking comprehensive performance analysis on their rides.</p></p>
