<p><p>The KN95 face mask market is primarily categorized into two types: flat-fold and cup style. Flat-fold masks are designed to collapse for easy storage and typically feature a flexible structure that adapts to facial contours, ensuring a snug fit. In contrast, cup style masks have a rigid structure that maintains its shape, providing additional space around the nose and mouth for comfort and breathability. Both types effectively filter airborne particles and are widely used in healthcare and non-medical settings.</p></p>
