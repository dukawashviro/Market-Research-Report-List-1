<p><p>Industrial branding labels encompass various market types crucial for product identification and marketing. Pressure-sensitive labels adhere easily to surfaces, while glue-applied labels require adhesive for bonding. Heat-shrink and stretch sleeve labels conform to container shapes, optimizing design space. In-mold labels integrate into products during manufacturing. Heat transfer labels use heat to transfer ink onto surfaces. Other types include non-shrink wraparound labels, which provide full coverage, gummed labels that require moisture to adhere, and foam labels offering a cushioning effect for fragile items.</p></p>
