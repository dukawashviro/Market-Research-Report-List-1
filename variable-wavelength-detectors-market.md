<p><ul><li>UV-Visible Detectors</li><li>Refractive Index Detectors</li><li>ELSD Detectors</li><li>Fluorescence Detectors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable wavelength detectors (VWD) in the market include several types, each serving specific analytical needs. UV-Visible detectors are commonly used for absorbing light in the UV or visible spectrum to quantify samples. Refractive index detectors measure changes in the refractive index of a sample, ideal for non-absorbing substances. Evaporative light scattering detectors (ELSD) detect analytes based on the light scattering principles of evaporated droplets. Fluorescence detectors utilize light absorption and emission properties, offering high sensitivity. Other emerging types provide additional detection methods tailored to specialized applications.</p></p>
