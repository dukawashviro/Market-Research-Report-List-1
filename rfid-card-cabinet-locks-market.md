<p><p>RFID card cabinet locks are categorized based on their operating range. Short-range RFID locks, typically functional within a few centimeters, offer heightened security for highly sensitive items and are often used in personal lockers. Middle-range locks bridge the gap, providing a versatile option suitable for various settings, balancing convenience and security for moderate-access environments like office spaces or educational institutions.</p></p>
