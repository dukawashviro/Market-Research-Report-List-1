<p><p>The Automatic Universal Hardness Tester market encompasses various testing methodologies used to assess material hardness. Rockwell Hardness measures resistance to deformation under load, while Vickers Hardness employs a diamond indenter for precise measurements across a range of materials. Brinell Hardness uses a steel ball to evaluate larger samples. Ball indentation testing focuses on softer materials, and carbon testing specifically assesses hardness in carbon-rich materials. Plastics testing examines the hardness of polymers, ensuring material performance in applications.</p></p>
