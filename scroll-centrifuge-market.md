<p><p>The scroll centrifuge market is divided into two main types: the two-phase scroll centrifuge and the three-phase scroll centrifuge. The two-phase scroll centrifuge separates solids from liquids, making it ideal for applications in wastewater treatment and industrial processes. In contrast, the three-phase scroll centrifuge effectively separates three components—solids, liquids, and two immiscible liquids—making it suitable for food processing and oil recovery. Both types enhance efficiency and facilitate the separation process in various industries, improving product quality and operational performance.</p></p>
