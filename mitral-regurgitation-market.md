<p><strong>The Mitral Regurgitation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Mitral Regurgitation</li><li>Secondary Mitral Regurgitation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mitral Regurgitation market is categorized into two primary types: Primary Mitral Regurgitation and Secondary Mitral Regurgitation. Primary Mitral Regurgitation is caused by intrinsic abnormalities of the mitral valve, leading to its improper closure. This type often stems from degenerative diseases, such as mitral valve prolapse. In contrast, Secondary Mitral Regurgitation results from structural heart changes, commonly due to conditions like heart failure or left ventricular dilation, where the valve itself is typically normal, but its function is compromised.</p></p>
