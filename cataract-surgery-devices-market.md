<p><p>The cataract surgery devices market comprises several key types, including Intraocular Lenses (IOLs), Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVDs), and phacoemulsification equipment. IOLs are artificial lenses implanted to restore vision after cataract removal. OVDs are used during surgery to maintain eye structure and protect tissues. Phacoemulsification equipment employs ultrasound energy to break up cataracts for easy removal. Together, these devices facilitate effective cataract surgery, enhancing patient outcomes and improving overall eye health.</p></p>
