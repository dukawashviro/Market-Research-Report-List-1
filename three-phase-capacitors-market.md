<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>Axial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase capacitor market can be divided into two main types: radial and axial capacitors. Radial capacitors feature leads coming from the bottom of the cylinder, making them suitable for circuit boards and applications with limited space. Axial capacitors have leads extending from both ends, allowing for easier installation in parallel configurations and better heat dissipation. Both types are essential in improving power factor and managing voltage levels in industrial and commercial electrical systems, enhancing overall efficiency.</p></p>
